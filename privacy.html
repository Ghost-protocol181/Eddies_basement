Privacy

Last updated July 2026.

What stays on your device

Favorites, recently viewed games, randomizer preferences, hidden games, reports, artwork status and basic usage events are stored in your browser. Eddie's Basement does not currently require an account or send this local information to a server.

External services

Game artwork and links may load from game publishers, stores or image services. Those services may receive standard browser information such as your IP address and user agent.

Your choices

You can clear Eddie's Basement data through your browser's site-data controls. Private browsing may limit or erase saved preferences.

Future changes

If accounts, advertising, email or third-party analytics are added, this page will be updated before those features launch.
