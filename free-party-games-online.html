What makes a good party pick?

Good party games do not require everyone to be a hardcore gamer. They should work for a mixed group, start fast, and give people something to laugh about or react to.

  • Drawing and guessing
  • Word games and trivia-style games
  • Social deduction with clear lobbies
  • Games that work in the same room or on a call
